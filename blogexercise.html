Let's make a happy little mountain now. Use what happens naturally, don't fight it. You can do anything here - the only pre-requisite is that it makes you happy.

You can spend all day playing with mountains. I will take some magic white, and a little bit of Vandyke brown and a little touch of yellow. You gotta think like a tree.
